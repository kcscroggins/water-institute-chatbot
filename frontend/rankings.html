Ranking Methodology

Research Impact Score (0-10) is calculated using four metrics:

H-Index (40%): Measures career publication impact
Field Citation Ratio (30%): Citations relative to field average (1.0 = average)
Total Citations (20%): Raw citation count
Grant Funding (10%): Research funding success

Data sourced from Dimensions.ai. Rankings are updated periodically.
