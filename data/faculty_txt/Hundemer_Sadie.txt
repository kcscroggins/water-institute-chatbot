Research Focus:
Dr. Hundemer's research addresses polarization in agriculture and natural resources. Key areas include stakeholder conflict resolution, political polarization of agricultural issues, water sustainability communication, and climate change communication. She specializes in "wicked problems" and mental modeling approaches. Her Ethics of Expertise research lab investigates scientists' public engagement goals and science's role in democracy.
