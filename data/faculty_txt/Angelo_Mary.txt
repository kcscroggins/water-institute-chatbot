Research Focus:
Professor Angelo is an Emeritus Samuel T. Dell Professor and Emeritus Professor of Law at the University of Florida Levin College of Law. Her scholarship draws on her background in biological sciences coupled with experience as an environmental lawyer. Her research explores the intersection of law and science, particularly focusing on how ecological sciences are used in, and can inform, environmental law. Areas of focus include pesticide law, endangered species law, water and wetlands law, sustainable agriculture, and regulation of genetically modified organisms.
