Research Focus:
Dr. Sharma specializes in precision water management for agriculture. His work emphasizes quantification of crop water use/evapotranspiration (ET), irrigation scheduling and requirements, and soil moisture sensing technologies. Key research areas include crop water productivity, climate impacts on agricultural output, and remote sensing applications for water resource decision-making.

Notable Projects:
- Florida Agricultural Soil Moisture Sensor Network
- Research on integrated water and fertilizer management affecting water quality
