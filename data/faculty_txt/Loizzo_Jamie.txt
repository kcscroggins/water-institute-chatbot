Research Focus:
Dr. Loizzo's research focuses on science communication and literacy, multimedia and mobile learning technologies, non-formal/informal science engagement, and arts-based research methodologies. She founded Streaming Science, a project-based learning and mobile electronic field trip program for 21st Century science communication education. She also developed the MOOCocracy platform for global science engagement and directs the SciLit Digital Research Group.
