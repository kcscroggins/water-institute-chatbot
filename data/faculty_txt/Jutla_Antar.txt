Research Focus:
Dr. Jutla's research centers on waterborne disease epidemiology and environmental engineering. Key areas include Vibrios and cholera transmission dynamics, flood forecasting and hydrological modeling, hydroepidemiology (intersection of hydrology and disease), and climate-water-disease linkages. He serves as Professor and Director of the USGS Water Resources Research Center at UF.
