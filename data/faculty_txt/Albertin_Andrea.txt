Research Focus:
Dr. Albertin is a Regional Specialized Agent in Water Resources, leading water-related extension education across the Florida Panhandle. Her extension programs focus on water quality protection by reducing point and non-point source pollution, promotion of Florida-Friendly Landscaping principles, septic system management, water conservation, and increasing use-efficiency in both agricultural and urban sectors. She is a regional expert in water resources issues including private well systems, drinking water quality, watershed pollution prevention, and agricultural best management practices.
