Research Focus:
Dr. Reyes develops enzyme stabilization strategies and biosensor technologies. His work encompasses enzyme stabilization to fabricate enzyme biosensors with extended operational life and biocatalytic processes for food and agricultural applications. Additional research areas include biofilm detection in water systems, cacao fermentation optimization, and supporting smallholder farmers in developing nations.
