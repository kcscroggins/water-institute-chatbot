Research Focus:
Ray Bodrey is County Extension Director and Extension Agent III for Gulf County in Northwest Florida, with a dual role as Marine Extension Agent III through the Florida Sea Grant College Program. His work translates UF research into on-the-ground practice, supporting producers, landowners, and coastal communities. His academic background and current Ph.D. work focus on soil and water sciences, with emphasis on soil health strategies especially after land conversion. He operates at the implementation interface of Florida water, soil, and conservation policy, supporting adoption of best management practices through UF/IFAS Extension.
