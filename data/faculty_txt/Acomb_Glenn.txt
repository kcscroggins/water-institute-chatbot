Research Focus:
Glenn Acomb's research focuses on green roof technology and sustainable stormwater management in sub-tropical and hurricane-prone climates. Current research activities involve developing new selections of green roof media and plant materials for sub-tropical projects, studying wind uplift on green roofs for hurricane regions' building codes, and evaluating the performance of design and plant selections in Low Impact Development (LID) stormwater devices in Florida.

Professional Bio:
Glenn Acomb is a Senior Lecturer in Landscape Architecture at the University of Florida specializing in sustainable site design, green roof technology, and stormwater management. His work addresses the unique challenges of implementing green infrastructure in Florida's sub-tropical climate and hurricane-prone regions.
