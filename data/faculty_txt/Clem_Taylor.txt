Research Focus:
Taylor Clem is a practical horticulture and landscape extension specialist who supports Florida-Friendly Landscaping best practices, Master Gardener volunteer coordination, residential and environmental horticulture outreach, and gardening, plant selection, and landscape health advice. His training combines landscape architecture and horticulture science, giving him a systems perspective on how landscapes function ecologically and how residents manage landscapes in ways that affect water, soil, and nutrient outcomes.
