Research Focus:
Dr. Kjelgren is a Professor Emeritus specializing in water use, water conservation, and plant-landscape relationships. His research applies horticultural and environmental science to quantify plant water use, irrigation efficiency, and sustainable landscape practices for urban and agricultural systems. He studies plant water stress responses, soil moisture dynamics, and water-wise landscape design, particularly for Florida and semi-tropical environments.
