Research Focus:
Dr. Yanong is an aquatic animal health veterinarian internationally recognized for his work on fish health, disease diagnosis, biosecurity, and health management in aquaculture and ornamental fish systems. He is especially known for translating veterinary science into practical guidance for producers, regulators, and aquatic animal professionals, bridging research, diagnostics, and on-the-ground management. He employs clinical and laboratory disease diagnostics, development of health management and biosecurity protocols, applied research and case-based studies, extension education and professional training, and collaboration with regulatory agencies and industry. Research systems include freshwater and marine aquaculture systems, ornamental fish industry with major emphasis, commercial producers hobbyists and aquatic animal professionals, and Florida and national aquaculture sectors.

- Publications addressing disease prevention biosecurity and health management

Policy Relevance:
Yanong's work supports aquaculture regulation and sustainability by providing science-based guidance that improves animal health, biosecurity, and compliance. He provides critical applied expertise that ensures aquaculture systems are biologically sustainable, economically viable, and compliant with animal health standards. His work influences disease prevention and biosecurity practices, aquatic animal welfare and health outcomes, and industry standards and regulatory guidance.
