Research Focus:
Dr. Peterson specializes in social ethics with emphasis on moral claims in everyday contexts. Key areas include social ethics, environmental ethics, religion and social change, animal studies, and Latin American religion and politics. She leads collaborative work on "Ethics in the Public Sphere," examining contentious public issues like police violence, climate change, and sexual violence.
