Research Focus:
Dr. Meyer's work sits at the intersection of microbial ecology and bioinformatics. Her research investigates microbial communities underpinning Florida and Caribbean habitats in need of conservation and/or restoration such as seagrass beds and coral reefs. She examines how microbiomes structure and impact ecosystem functions.
