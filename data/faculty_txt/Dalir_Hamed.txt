Research Focus:
Dr. Dalir's research focuses on Mid-IR Photonics, On-chip Light Sources, Classical and Quantum Sensors, Ising Machines, AI Accelerators, and Photonic Integrated Circuits. His CHIP Lab develops integrated systems using engineered phase-change materials and optical elements for efficient, high-speed, low-power domain transformation. In collaboration with TSMC, he advances Indium Tin Oxide technology integration on silicon platforms.
