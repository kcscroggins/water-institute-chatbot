Research Focus:
Dr. Ampatzidis is a Professor in Agricultural and Biological Engineering who leads a research and extension precision agriculture engineering program at the Southwest Florida Research and Education Center (SWFREC) in Immokalee. His program focuses on the design, development, and testing of sensors and control systems for optimal management of inputs, resources, and products. Research areas include mechanical harvesting of specialty crops, mechatronics, artificial intelligence, machine vision, precision agriculture, smart machines (smart spraying technologies), UAVs, and machine systems. His team develops AI-based systems to detect, distinguish, and categorize agricultural objects including citrus trees, fruit, flowers, weeds, and pests.

Notable Technologies:
- Agroview: Cloud-based application to process, analyze, and visualize UAV-collected data for precision agriculture
- Agrosense: AI-enabled digital agriculture tool for grower decision-making
