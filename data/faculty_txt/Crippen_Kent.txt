Research Focus:
Dr. Crippen's research examines how networked learning technologies can create inclusive STEM education pathways. His work addresses the chronic problem of under-representation of specific populations in STEM through cyberlearning environments. He investigates how design-based interventions can tackle persistent challenges in STEM education.

Notable Positions:
