Research Focus:
Danny Coenen is an instructional associate professor and undergraduate coordinator in the School of Natural Resources and Environment (SNRE). His primary responsibilities focus on teaching environmental science courses and supporting the undergraduate environmental science program. His teaching emphasizes systems thinking, environmental literacy, and critical evaluation of complex environmental issues using interdisciplinary approaches that integrate physical, biological, and socio-ecological perspectives. His earlier research focused on lake temperature trends as climate indicators in Florida ecosystems.
