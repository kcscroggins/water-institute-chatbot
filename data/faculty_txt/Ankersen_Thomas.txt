Research Focus:
Professor Ankersen is an Emeritus Legal Skills Professor and Emeritus Director of the Conservation Clinic at the University of Florida Levin College of Law. His work bridges legal education and practical environmental law, focusing on domestic and international environmental law and policy, water law, coastal and marine law, and protection of biological diversity. He has worked extensively in Florida as well as in Latin America and the Caribbean, having taught and worked in 30+ countries. He is fluent in Spanish, supporting his extensive international engagement.
