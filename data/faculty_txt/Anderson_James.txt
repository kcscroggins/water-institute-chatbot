Research Focus:
Dr. Anderson is an expert in natural resource economics, fisheries and aquaculture economics, seafood markets and international trade, and sustainable food systems. His work includes the development of performance indicators for fisheries and aquaculture, economic modeling of seafood sectors, and analysis of how aquaculture growth and fisheries management reforms affect global seafood markets. He is affiliated with the Food Systems Institute at UF.
