Subject Areas:

Research Focus:
Dr. Babaeian's work centers on mass and energy distribution and transport in soil, toward enhancing management and sustainability of environment. His research includes characterizing land surface variables using ground and remote sensing technologies, multiscale observations and data fusion techniques for soil physical properties, physics-informed and data-driven modeling approaches, and climate-related dynamics of water and energy cycles.

Notable Publications:
- Remote sensing applications for soil hydraulic property retrieval (2015)
- Comprehensive reviews on soil moisture remote sensing across multiple platforms (2019)
- Optical trapezoid modeling for Sentinel-2 and Landsat-8 observations (2017)
- Shortwave infrared imaging for rapid soil hydraulic estimation (2021)
