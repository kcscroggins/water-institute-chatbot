Research Focus:
Caitlin Justesen is an Extension Agent specializing in county-level Extension education and outreach, particularly working with 4-H youth development, family and consumer sciences, and community engagement. Her role emphasizes education, capacity building, and support for agricultural and community stakeholders in areas that touch on livestock, natural resources, and family well-being. She delivers science-based education and technical assistance to community and producer audiences in Suwannee County.

Extension Outputs:
- County newsletters and program guides on 4-H programming and family sciences
- Workshops, training sessions, and field days for local audiences
- Technical resources on youth leadership, safe food handling, and community resilience
- Community engagement initiatives supporting agricultural and natural resource connections
