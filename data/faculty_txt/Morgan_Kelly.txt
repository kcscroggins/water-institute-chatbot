Research Focus:

Policy Relevance:
Dr. Morgan produces mechanistic science and BMP guidance that informs nutrient management policy and agricultural practices to balance crop productivity with minimized nutrient loss and improved water quality outcomes. His research directly addresses nutrient cycling, soil fertility, and nutrient loss pathways in agricultural systems, including nitrogen leaching and phosphorus mobility. His work elucidates mechanisms controlling nutrient availability and movement in soils (adsorption/desorption, mineralization, leaching), critical for understanding nutrient retention and loss to water. His Extension recommendations and involvement with BMP guidance support policy compliance and nutrient management regulation at county and state levels.
