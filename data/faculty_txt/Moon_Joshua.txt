Subject Areas:

Research Focus:
Dr. Moon's laboratory concentrates on designing polymer materials for membrane separations. The group's mission is to overcome Grand Challenges in clean water and energy production through advanced polymer synthesis and experimental characterization. Key application areas include carbon capture, hydrogen production, hydrocarbon purification, water treatment, and environmental remediation.
