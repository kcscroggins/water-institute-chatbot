Research Focus:
Dr. Martens-Habbena's work sits at the intersection of microbiology, molecular biology and biogeochemistry. His research focuses on understanding how microorganisms drive biogeochemical cycles and their applications for human and environmental benefit. He investigates global change and sea level rise impacts on South Florida communities, explores microbial solutions to environmental challenges, and examines ecological interactions between microorganisms in complex ecosystems.
