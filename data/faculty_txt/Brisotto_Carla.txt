Research Focus:
Carla Brisotto is an institute-level research administrator and program leader whose work focuses on building, coordinating, and scaling interdisciplinary resilience research at the university-government-industry interface. As Associate Director of FIBER, she plays a central role in translating research into deployable resilience solutions, coordinating across engineering, planning, policy, and environmental sciences, managing large-scale projects and stakeholder relationships, and supporting grants, partnerships, and strategic initiatives. Her functional expertise includes program and portfolio management, cross-disciplinary team building, government and community engagement, applied research deployment, and strategic planning for resilience initiatives.
