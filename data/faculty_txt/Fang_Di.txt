Research Focus:
Dr. Fang's research focuses on the intersection of food policy, nutrition, and health economics, particularly in evaluating programs that influence food purchasing and health outcomes. She explores the intersections between nutrition, environment, and health using applied economics. Her research examined food security and mental health during COVID-19, identifying correlations between food insecurity and anxiety/depression.
