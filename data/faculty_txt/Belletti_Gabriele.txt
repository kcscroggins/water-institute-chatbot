Research Focus:
Dr. Belletti's research encompasses environmental humanities, 20th and 21st-century poetics, migration studies, and literary connections between Italy and France. He is Principal Investigator of the SHINE Project (Sciences Humanities Intelligence Nurturing Emotions), an interdisciplinary initiative exploring how poetic texts and immersive virtual environments affect emotional engagement and ecological awareness. The project integrates AI, literary analysis, neuroscience, and environmental humanities to foster empathy and pro-environmental behaviors.

Notable Work:
