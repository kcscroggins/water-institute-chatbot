Research Focus:
Dr. Marcos Marin is an internationally recognized Hispanic linguist, philologist, and medievalist. His work focuses on the history of the Spanish language, Old Spanish and Old Portuguese texts, Romance philology, historical lexicography, and medieval Iberian literature and culture. He produces foundational scholarly resources including critical editions, historical linguistic analysis, and lexicographical contributions that support teaching, research, and cultural heritage preservation.

Notable Publications:
- Historical linguistics and Romance philology articles tracing language change and medieval dialects in Spanish and Portuguese
- Lexicographical and textual editions of medieval works - critical editions of medieval Spanish and Portuguese texts
- Studies on Old Spanish and Old Portuguese language structures - syntax, morphology, and historical phonology
- Humanities scholarship on language contact and medieval Iberian culture
- Monographs, critical editions, and journal articles in linguistics and medieval studies

Water Institute Relevance:
While Dr. Marcos Marin's expertise is in the humanities (linguistics and philology) rather than environmental science, his work supports humanities research infrastructure (historical corpora, lexicographical standards) that underpins education and cultural policy.
