Research Focus:
Bradley Burbaugh is the County Extension Director and Extension Agent II for UF/IFAS Extension Clay County. His work centers on applied agriculture, natural resources, and community education, translating university research into practical guidance for producers, land managers, and local stakeholders. He operates at the ground level where policy gets executed, serving as a trusted intermediary between regulators and producers, between research and real-world constraints, and between conservation goals and economic realities.
