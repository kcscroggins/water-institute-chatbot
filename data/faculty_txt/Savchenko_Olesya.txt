Research Focus:
Dr. Savchenko employs experimental economics and non-market valuation to examine the impact of human behavioral changes on natural systems and develop agri-environmental policies. Her current work addresses water quality adoption, aquatic invasive species management, harmful algal blooms' economic effects, and sustainable food consumption patterns.
