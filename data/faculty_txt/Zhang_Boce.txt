Research Focus:
Dr. Zhang specializes in food safety and food microbiology with research integrating microbial omics, machine learning, nanotechnology, and biointerface science to study the mechanisms of pathogen persistence. His current projects include AI-enabled sensor arrays for pathogen detection, nanopore sequencing for surveillance, and biofilm intervention strategies.
