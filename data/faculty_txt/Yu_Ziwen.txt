Research Focus:
Dr. Yu is an agricultural and biological engineer whose work focuses on precision agriculture, sensing, and data-driven methods to improve agricultural management and environmental performance. He is known for integrating engineering, sensing technologies, and analytical methods to support efficient resource use and decision-making in agricultural systems. He employs sensor development and deployment for field-scale monitoring, data acquisition processing and analytics, engineering design and system integration, modeling and decision-support tools for agriculture, and field experiments and applied system evaluation. Research systems include crop production systems relevant to Florida and the southeastern U.S., managed agricultural landscapes requiring fine-scale monitoring, and systems where sensing and analytics improve management outcomes.

- Engineering solutions that improve efficiency and sustainability in production systems
- Publications in agricultural engineering and precision agriculture journals
- Sensor-based monitoring and system design for optimized agricultural practices

Policy Relevance:
Yu's work supports evidence-based agricultural management and sustainability by providing engineering tools that improve monitoring, efficiency, and decision-making. He contributes engineering innovations that translate data into actionable insight, enabling more precise and efficient agricultural management. His work influences sensing and monitoring infrastructure, data-driven agricultural decision-making, and engineering design for sustainable production systems.
