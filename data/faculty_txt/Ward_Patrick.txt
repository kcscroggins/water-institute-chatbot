Research Focus:
Patrick Ward specializes in sustainable development with emphasis on agriculture in low- and middle-income countries. His work integrates interdisciplinary approaches to evaluate policy interventions addressing environmental and developmental challenges. Current research includes rural financial market development, agricultural risk management, soil fertility and conservation, rural mechanization markets, and water resource management. Fieldwork spans South Asia, China, Malawi, and Kenya.
