Research Focus:
Dr. Anderson's research program focuses on sustainability in the aquaculture industry, examining disease management, food security, and conservation finance. His work focuses on the stewardship of marine resources in the production of seafood from aquaculture and fisheries. He has explored best management practices for disease management in salmon aquaculture in Chile and examined how top-down policies for antibiotic use could control disease outbreaks.

Notable Publications:
- Environmental, economic, and social sustainability in aquaculture (Nature Communications, 2024)
- "Finer economic margins for farmed salmon" (Aquaculture)
- Working papers on spatial-dynamic externalities in Chilean aquaculture and option pricing in cap-and-trade fisheries markets
