Research Focus:
Michelle Atkinson provides community outreach, education, and research-based programming related to environmental horticulture, sustainable landscaping practices, and water resource protection. She develops and delivers workshops and training for pest control professionals, lawn and landscape industries, golf courses, homeowners, and community groups. Her program areas include landscape management, urban and community pest management, and pesticide safety training and testing. She oversees a residential Mobile Irrigation Lab focused on encouraging outdoor water conservation and best management practices in the landscape. Michelle works closely with local stakeholders, homeowner associations, and professionals to implement science-based environmental horticulture solutions that protect water quality and conserve vital resources. She has co-authored UF/IFAS publications on horticulture and stormwater pond management.
