Top Agriculture / Irrigation / BMP Researchers at UF Water Institute
====================================================================

These researchers are the leading experts on agricultural water management,
irrigation science, and best management practices (BMPs) at the UF Water Institute,
ranked by their topic-specific research (grants, publications, leadership roles).

---

1. Kati W. Migliaccio - Agricultural and Biological Engineering
   Expertise: Irrigation science, water conservation, precision irrigation, watershed modeling
   Key Work: Professor and Dean; developer of Smart Irrigation Apps; ranked #1 in
   Geoinformatics; research on evapotranspiration-based irrigation scheduling;
   UF/IFAS fact sheets on irrigation best practices; Indian River Lagoon nutrient research
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=pEd15xAAAAAJ&hl=en

2. Rafael Munoz-Carpena - Agricultural and Biological Engineering
   Expertise: Agricultural water quality, vegetative filter strips, nutrient runoff modeling
   Key Work: Distinguished Professor; developer of VFSMOD (Vegetative Filter Strip
   Modeling System); research on BMP effectiveness for sediment and nutrient control;
   Lake Okeechobee and Kissimmee River water quality modeling
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=8-GYxFAAAAAJ&hl=en

3. Sanjay Shukla - Agricultural and Biological Engineering
   Expertise: Agricultural watershed management, non-point source pollution, stormwater
   Key Work: Research on agricultural detention systems for nutrient treatment;
   $499K EPA grant on ecosystem services in Florida ranchlands;
   tomato production water sustainability research
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=nID94UcAAAAJ&hl=en

4. Lincoln Zotarelli - Horticultural Sciences
   Expertise: Vegetable irrigation, crop water use, nutrient management
   Key Work: Research on irrigation scheduling for vegetable crops;
   Extension publications on crop water requirements and irrigation efficiency
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=bUU6zcwAAAAJ&hl=en

5. Michael Dukes - Agricultural and Biological Engineering
   Expertise: Irrigation efficiency, smart irrigation controllers, turfgrass irrigation
   Key Work: Research on water-saving irrigation technologies;
   smart irrigation controller development and evaluation
