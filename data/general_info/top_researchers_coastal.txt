Top Coastal / Marine Researchers at UF Water Institute
======================================================

These researchers are the leading experts on coastal systems, marine science,
and ocean dynamics at the UF Water Institute, ranked by their topic-specific
research (grants, publications, leadership roles).

---

1. Arnoldo Valle-Levinson - Civil and Coastal Engineering
   Expertise: Coastal hydrodynamics, estuarine circulation, sea-level variability, tidal dynamics
   Key Work: Ranked #1 in Oceanography at Water Institute; leads research on estuarine
   exchange flows, Gulf of Mexico transport processes, storm surge dynamics;
   $403K NSF grant on storm surge perturbations
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2lZZB4oAAAAJ&hl=en

2. Andrew Altieri - Environmental Engineering Sciences
   Expertise: Coral reef ecosystems, ocean deoxygenation, coastal restoration, nature-based solutions
   Key Work: Interim Director, Center for Coastal Solutions; research on Caribbean coral
   reef deoxygenation (82 citations in Nature Communications); leads nature-based solutions program
   Website: http://www.altierilab.org/
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=3bUXmwcAAAAJ&hl=en

3. Frank Asche - Food and Resource Economics
   Expertise: Marine economics, fisheries economics, aquaculture economics
   Key Work: Ranked #6 overall at Water Institute; internationally recognized for
   seafood market analysis and sustainable fisheries economics
   Website: https://ffgs.ifas.ufl.edu/faculty/asche-frank/
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=hF8FTTIAAAAJ&hl=en

4. Kai Lorenzen - Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences
   Expertise: Fisheries science, marine stock assessment, fisheries enhancement
   Key Work: Distinguished Professor; developer of "Lorenzen curve" used globally
   in stock assessment; research on sustainable fisheries management
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=FIzWeRAAAAAJ&hl=en

5. Laura Reynolds - Soil, Water and Ecosystem Sciences
   Expertise: Coastal wetlands, seagrass ecosystems, blue carbon, coastal restoration
   Key Work: Research on seagrass ecology and coastal ecosystem resilience;
   mangrove and salt marsh dynamics
