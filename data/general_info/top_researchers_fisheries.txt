Top Fisheries / Aquatic Ecology Researchers at UF Water Institute
================================================================

These researchers are the leading experts on fisheries science, fish population
dynamics, and aquatic ecology at the UF Water Institute, ranked by their
topic-specific research (grants, publications, leadership roles).

---

1. Micheal S. Allen - School of Forest, Fisheries, and Geomatics Sciences
   Expertise: Fish population dynamics, recreational fisheries management, habitat restoration
   Key Work: Director of Nature Coast Biological Station (NCBS); research on fishing
   mortality effects, harvest slot strategies, coastal habitat restoration (living shorelines);
   simulation modeling for fisheries management decisions
   Website: https://ffgs.ifas.ufl.edu/faculty/allen-mike/
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=mzmgWusAAAAJ&hl=en

2. Kai Lorenzen - Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences
   Expertise: Fisheries science, stock assessment, fisheries enhancement, population modeling
   Key Work: Distinguished Professor of Integrative Fisheries Science; developer of
   "Lorenzen curve" for natural mortality estimation (used globally in stock assessments);
   research on sustainable fisheries and hatchery management
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=FIzWeRAAAAAJ&hl=en

3. Frank Asche - Food and Resource Economics
   Expertise: Fisheries economics, seafood markets, aquaculture economics
   Key Work: Ranked #6 overall at Water Institute; internationally recognized for
   economic analysis of fisheries and aquaculture; sustainable seafood research
   Website: https://ffgs.ifas.ufl.edu/faculty/asche-frank/
   Google Scholar: https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=hF8FTTIAAAAJ&hl=en

4. William Pine - Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences
   Expertise: Fish ecology, population assessment, conservation
   Key Work: Research on fish population dynamics and conservation;
   freshwater and marine fisheries assessment methods

5. Debra Murie - Fisheries and Aquatic Sciences
   Expertise: Fish biology, age and growth, fisheries assessment
   Key Work: Research on fish life history, stock assessment methods,
   and sustainable fisheries management
